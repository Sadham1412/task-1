T20 World Cup final: India defeat New Zealand by 96 runs for third title

Bumrah's stunning bowling sealed India's record-breaking win at home after Samson and Abhishek's batting fireworks.


Heartiest congratulations to Team India on a brilliant victory over England and a well-deserved place in the ICC T20 World Cup 2026 Final! Your determination, teamwork, and fearless performance have filled the nation with pride.

India's victory in the ICC T20 World Cup 2026 final against New Zealand was a testament to their exceptional performance and teamwork. The match, held at home, showcased the brilliance of players like Bumrah, Samson, and Abhishek, who played pivotal roles in securing the win.

Bumrah's stunning bowling was instrumental in sealing India's record-breaking win, while Samson and Abhishek's batting fireworks provided the necessary momentum for the team. The victory not only adds a third title to India's cricketing achievements but also cements their status as a dominant force in international cricket.
